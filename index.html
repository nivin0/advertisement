Home

                                   Welcome to Agar Milk – Pure Goodness in Every Drop

At Agar Milk, we are committed to delivering pure, farm-fresh dairy products straight from our Relance Garden farm in 
Kadampadi to your doorstep. With a deep respect for nature and a focus on quality, we bring you nutritious and delicious 
dairy essentials like fresh milk, curd, ghee, and more – all produced in a clean, well-managed environment. Our cows are 
raised with care in a natural, stress-free setting, ensuring the milk they produce is rich in flavor and packed with 
essential nutrients. Every drop of Agar Milk reflects our promise of purity, freshness, and trust. Whether you’re a 
family seeking healthy,chemical-free dairy or someone who simply appreciates the taste of fresh farm products, Agar Milk 
is here to serve you with the best.
Thank you for visiting – we welcome you to explore our offerings and become a part of the Agar Milk family.

About us

-Agar milk private limited, Established in the Year 2016 for Making Milk and Milk Products. The Company is focussed
Entirely in Delivering the Nutritional Value of Milk in its Best form to the Customers.

-Agar Milk is the First to Adopt the Latest innovations in the Milk Processing Technology to deliver the Milk Proteins in
its best form.

-Re Started Milk and Milk products production in 2025, Currently Handling ABout 70 Litres of Milk Per day. 

-As we have the Best Quality and Consumer Satisfaction in the Commercial Milk Segment, We are one of the fast growing
dairy Companies in Coimbatore. Also, We are Expanding our Business into the Consumer Segment in Increasing the House
Delivery Locations. We have adapted several New Strategies in Giving Better Quality of Service and Better Confidence
on the products through several initiatives. 

-As a Vision., 2025 We have Plans to Increase the Coverage by 30% Within 2025 and 50% More Coverage by 2027.
